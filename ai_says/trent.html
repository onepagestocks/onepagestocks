Company Description & Market Presence

Trent Limited, a subsidiary of the Tata Group, is a prominent Indian retail company operating a diverse portfolio of retail concepts:

  • Westside: Flagship fashion retail chain (apparel, footwear, home furnishings).
  • Zudio: Value fashion concept (attractive price points).
  • Star: Hypermarket/supermarket chain (staples, fresh produce, electronics).
  • Utsa: Modern Indian woman's lifestyle destination.
  • SAMOH: Elevated occasional wear concept.

Strong and expanding retail footprint across India with over 1,000 fashion stores (248 Westside, 757 Zudio as of April 2025). Operates both online and physical stores.
