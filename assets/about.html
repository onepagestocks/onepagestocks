So we designed a method to give some sanity among the madness and we believe if you follow it with keen (give it a few minutes) for a couple of weeks you will realize. It's actually simple.

The Method!

As you would have guessed by now that this report is to help a trading system which is personally suited for you. If you have a job or business and can spend a little time growing your hard-earned cash.

The results are based on an automated scan. The scan is nothing new and is publicly available. Following are the criteria for the scan:

“We want movement in stocks, remember “Stock is right if it moves”. This can be identified with momentum but we don’t want to enter when everyone in the village including your neighbor’s dog has finished entering. Something that has recently started moving more than the markets. Large volumes after a long silence is one sign and other signs are pattern breakouts.”

This report narrows down stocks from scans and lists them in a page with additional information, so that you don’t have to manually look through all the stocks and identify them. It does the following:

  1. Collect all data from the market from the time they have existed.
  2. Run the scan based on volume and other things to narrow them down.
  3. Count the number of such pop ups in the previous 20 days.
  4. Count the number of such pop ups in the previous 9 months.
  5. Collate all results in a one page with following information:
    • Analysts ratings and number of analysts from yahoo.
    • Industry and a brief about stock.
    • A full blown interactive chart of the stock to quickly skim through if any patterns are visible or likable.
    • Screener and google stocks link for further fundamental analysis like cash flow, revenue, latest news etc.
  6. All the above in a single page so that with a single scroll what looks good can be quickly investigated further and decided upon.

Even if there is weekly one to two new stocks that you want to invest in, your portfolio grows in a year from 50 to 100 stocks and thus reduces risk; it greatly diversifies your portfolio. This way it takes around half an hour a week to keep up with the stocks that matter for us and make decisions based on that.

Limitations

  • We do not vouch for any stock here as we are not experts. If you don’t trust anyone else when it comes to investing your money then this is a good report to spend 30 mins of your weekend afternoon. And it comes free…
  • We do not claim that we have made xx profits and that’s why you should trust us, rather we say these are some 100 narrowed down stocks and you can quickly eyeball this to find if you see a gem in it. How is it narrowed down - it’s simple, whichever has seen a huge bump in their volumes recently. That’s it! It encapsulates all other technical indicators for us, even the breakouts that matter. Think about it!
  • It doesn’t provide any exit or target to any of the stocks, you may see them for some stocks but we don’t trust them and they come again from yahoo stocks.
  • It doesn’t provide any predictions of the future as it can’t be predicted. Rather it’s to decide where you should put your money today.
  • The method is semi - automatic because looking at fundamental factors is pretty subjective, also everyone has different investing/trading style, how it is to be used is up to you.
